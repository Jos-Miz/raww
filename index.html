About Enchanted Florals

Welcome to Enchanted Florals, where flowers aren’t just flowers, they’re enchanted. We opened our doors in 2024 with a goal, to share our love of flowers with our community.

Our shop is tucked away in the heart of Floraltown. Whether you’re here to pick out a unique bouquet, join one of our fun DIY workshops, or just browse through our garden-inspired boutique, we want you to feel like you’re stepping into an enchanted garden.

Our Mission

At Enchanted Florals, we care about the little things, like working with local growers to bring you the freshest, most beautiful blooms all while helping you create arrangements that are as unique as you are.
